About Us

Assurity

As a mutual organization, Assurity was founded on the simple concept of people coming together to support each other in moments of need. We help people through difficult times by providing affordable insurance protection that is easy to understand and buy – we’re there when our customers need us. We all share in the future we create, and Assurity believes in using our business as a force for good.
