About QuickStart

QuickStart is the easiest way to modernize your business and start selling online – backed by a carrier that’s committed to putting your needs and goals first. It’s everything you need to process and bind a policy, all in one place, with the robust options you and your clients expect.

Platform Advantages

Two ways to sell

Whether you need a direct-to consumer website, an agent-led e-app or both, we have flexible, turnkey options for your business.

Complete customization

Everythingis built to fit your business: the look, feel, product offerings and even the states you want to sell in.

Easy to use

A streamlined interface, simple language and functionality for both mobile and desktop make QuickStart a hassle-free experience for you and your clients with compliance guaranteed.

Life and health insurance
