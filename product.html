Term Life

Assurity Term Life Insurance provides your clients with fast, affordable level term life insurance coverage with living benefits. You can customize coverage with in-demand options like the Return of Premium Rider.

Product Details

  • Term Periods: 10, 15, 20 or 30 years
  • Issue Ages: 18-75, depending on selected term period
  • No medical exams / Guaranteed Renewable
  • Issue Amounts: $25,000 - $500,000
  • Convertibility: Convertible to permanent coverage

Target Markets

  • Young Families
  • Established Families
  • Millennials
  • Business Owners
  • People searching for term coverage with optional add-ons

Underwriting made easy

Get underwriting decisions fast – all applicants are considered for approval at the time of application. We provide instant decisions on up to 50 percent of applicants – and if clients aren’t instantly approved, they’ll be seamlessly transition to traditional underwriting.

Income Protection

ncome Protection Individual Disability Income Insurance provides highly flexible disability insurance, with options for both Accident & Sickness and Accident-Only coverage. It also features wide selections of benefit and elimination periods, in-demand riders like the Stay-at-Home Spouse Rider and consideration for multiple incomes.

Product Highlights

  • Issue Ages: 18-60, age last birthday as of issue date
  • Occupation Classes:4A, 3A, 3A, 1A
  • Maximum Weekly Benefits: $50 - $600 weekly for self-employed or commissioned salesperson; $50 - $1,000 for W-2 employees
  • Benefit Periods:13-week, 26-week, 1-year, 2-year
  • Elimination Periods:0, 7, 14, 30, 60 or 90 days, depending on selected benefit period

Target Markets
